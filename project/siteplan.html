Project Site Plan

Site Name

EcoCycle Nigeria

This name represents a website focused on promoting recycling and sustainable waste management practices in Nigeria.

Site Purpose

The site provides information and resources on recycling and sustainable waste management practices in Nigeria.

Services:

  • Recycling guides and tips
  • Directory of recycling centers and facilities
  • Blog on sustainable living and environmental conservation

Scenarios

What are the best practices for recycling plastic waste in Nigeria?

Where can I find a recycling center near me?
